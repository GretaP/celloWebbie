I consider teaching on of the most important things I do as a cellist, and by far the one that holds the most responsibility.

I love teaching, and my students love coming to lessons. I have developed a unique teaching style since I started teaching in 2004 which is tailored to each student. I do not hold strictly to "Suzuki" "Traditional" or "Starker" pedagogies; I take what I like best from what I have learned and pass it on.

I teach privately in Boulder Colorado and accept students age 4 and up (adults included).
