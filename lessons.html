Teachers make a huge difference - for better or worse - in each student they work with. This is especially true in music lessons where technique is extremely details and of utmost importance, and where progress is slow and it can be hard to stay inspired.

To help you choose the right teacher, I offer reduced price trial lessons. I also will chat with you on the phone or over email for free to discuss my teaching methodologies, your needs, and anything you want to know about lessons.
